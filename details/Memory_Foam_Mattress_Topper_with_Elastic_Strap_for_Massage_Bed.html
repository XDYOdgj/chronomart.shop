Memory Foam Mattress Topper for Ultimate Comfort and Support

This high-quality mattress topper features a 4cm thick memory foam layer, designed to enhance comfort and provide exceptional pressure relief. Whether you're a therapist or client, you'll appreciate the soft yet supportive feel that helps reduce strain and improve relaxation. With a multi-layer construction that includes latex for durability, soybean fiber for breathability, and high-density sponge for firm support, it's built to last and offer a premium experience.

Perfect for beauty salons, massage parlors, and spas, it adds a touch of elegance while protecting your massage table from dirt, wear, and daily use.
