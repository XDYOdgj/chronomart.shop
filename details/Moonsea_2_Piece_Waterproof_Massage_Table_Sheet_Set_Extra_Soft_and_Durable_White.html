Moonsea's 2-Piece Waterproof Massage Table Sheet Set

Enhance your massage practice with a set that blends comfort, durability, and hygiene. The 2-piece design includes a soft microfiber top layer for a luxurious feel, a quilted polyester filling to provide extra cushioning, a waterproof middle layer to protect against spills and sweat, and an additional fabric bottom layer for stability. This combination ensures that both clients and therapists enjoy a safe, clean, and supportive experience every time.

Why Choose This Set?

  • Designed for ease of use, fit most standard massage tables with rounded or squared corners
  • Includes a 72" x 33" flat pad and a 14" x 13" x 5" face cradle cover for full coverage and comfort

Perfect for Every Massage Setup
