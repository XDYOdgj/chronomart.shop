ForPro Professional Fleece Massage Table Warmer

Elevate your massage sessions with this premium table warmer that combines comfort, durability, and advanced functionality for professionals. Whether you're in a spa, salon, or wellness center, this essential tool ensures a soothing and personalized treatment for every client.

Perfectly Tailored Comfort

Experience the luxury of 9 adjustable heat settings, ranging from 85°F to 133°F, to create the ideal temperature for any massage. The intuitive LED controller makes it easy to set the perfect time, from 1 to 9 hours, ensuring you deliver consistent warmth to enhance the client's relaxation.

Designed for Professionals

This extra-large massage table warmer measures 31\" x 72\" and fits all standard tables without any hassle. The detachable 6-foot power cord provides flexibility and portability, while the soft fleece cover made from hypoallergenic polyester ensures a safe, skin-friendly experience for sensitive clients.

Easy to Maintain

Enjoy peace of mind with a machine-washable fleece cover that’s simple to clean. The elastic corner straps prevent slipping and bunching, keeping the warmer in place throughout your session for a seamless and comfortable treatment.

Upgrade Your Massage Experience

Whether you're offering deep tissue, Swedish, or any other type of massage, this table pad delivers consistent heat and comfort to help your clients unwind. It's the perfect addition to professional massage equipment, enhancing both service quality and client satisfaction.
